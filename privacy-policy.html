Privacy Policy

PR Primespot values reader trust. We collect only limited information necessary to operate our newsroom, improve journalism, and communicate with readers. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, certain technical information such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, and interaction data may be collected automatically to improve performance and user experience.

If you contact us directly through forms, newsletters, or email communications, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide.

  • Email address for newsletters
  • Contact form submissions
  • Feedback and inquiries
  • Analytics and usage data

How Information Is Used

  • To improve website performance and newsroom operations
  • To understand reader engagement and interests
  • To respond to messages, inquiries, or correction requests
  • To provide updates and newsletter communications
  • To maintain website security and prevent misuse

We do not sell personal information to advertisers or third parties.

Cookies and Analytics

PR Primespot uses cookies and analytics technologies to understand how readers interact with our journalism and improve site functionality. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics may process limited anonymized usage data under their own privacy policies.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Our platform may display advertisements, sponsored content, or affiliate links. Advertising partners may use cookies to provide relevant ads and measure performance.

We work only with reputable partners and do not directly sell reader data.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of personal data.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

While no online platform can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to maintain secure systems and responsible practices.
