FBI Says It Foiled Plot Targeting White House UFC Event Attended by Trump

Federal authorities say they disrupted an alleged plot involving drones and firearms targeting a UFC event at the White House attended by President Donald Trump and other high-profile guests.

Federal authorities say they disrupted an alleged plot targeting a UFC event held on the White House grounds during celebrations marking President Donald Trump’s 80th birthday and the 250th anniversary of the Declaration of Independence. FBI Director Kash Patel said investigators uncovered the plan before it could be carried out and detained multiple suspects as part of a coordinated operation. According to court documents and statements from federal officials, the alleged conspiracy involved discussions about using explosive-laden drones and firearms during the UFC Freedom 250 event on the South Lawn of the White House. Authorities said the operation was disrupted before the suspects were capable of carrying out an attack. The event drew national attention because it featured President Donald Trump, UFC executives, political figures and thousands of attendees. Officials stressed that no attack occurred and that there was never an immediate danger to spectators once the investigation was underway. The FBI described the case as a serious national security matter and said investigations remain ongoing.

Court Documents Detail Drone and Firearms Allegations

Prosecutors allege that members of the group discussed launching explosive-equipped drones toward areas near the White House before using firearms against people fleeing the scene. Investigators said the plans were shared through encrypted messaging platforms and included discussions about logistics, escape routes and potential targets. Federal authorities stated that at least five individuals were arrested across multiple states, while investigators continue examining whether additional people were involved. Court filings indicate that the group allegedly exchanged maps, operational details and plans related to the event. Officials said the conspiracy was still in the planning stages and had not reached operational readiness. Investigators nevertheless treated the threat seriously because of the scale of the event and the presence of high-profile attendees. The suspects face federal charges that include conspiracy and weapons-related offenses. Court proceedings are expected to continue in the coming weeks.

Investigation Began After Tip to Authorities

The investigation reportedly accelerated after a family member of one suspect alerted authorities about concerning behavior and weapons purchases. According to federal officials, that information helped investigators identify communications and activities connected to the alleged conspiracy. In the aftermath, the FBI worked with law enforcement agencies across multiple states to locate suspects and gather evidence. The operation involved extensive surveillance, the collection of digital evidence and the analysis of online communications, authorities said. Officials said the prevention of what could have been a dangerous scenario was a result of reporting suspicious activity and cooperation between agencies. They emphasized the importance of the public reporting suspicious behavior or signs of extremist activity. The motives of the suspects and any larger networks that may be linked to the alleged plot are still under investigation.

Security Concerns Continue to Be a National Issue

The incident underscores the continuing concerns over the security of large public gatherings attended by political leaders and other high-profile individuals. Federal agencies have increased attention on threats involving drones, online radicalization and coordinated attacks planned through encrypted communications. Officials say technological advances have created new challenges for security services responsible for protecting large gatherings. President Trump told reporters that he had not been fully briefed on the details of the alleged plot at the time of his initial public comments. Federal authorities have continued providing updates as the investigation progresses. While the UFC event proceeded without incident, the arrests and court filings have drawn significant national attention. Charges are expected to be filed by prosecutors, and investigators will continue to dig into the full breadth of the alleged conspiracy. It's a closely watched case.
